ABOUT US

Maple Leaf Construction is an industry leader in the construction field.We utilize advanced technology and state of the art equipment to help ensure the products and services we deliver are done quickly and of the highest quality.


Whether it be grade controlled electronics for asphalt pavers and concrete finishers or GPS controlled excavation and base equipment, Maple Leaf Construction’s team will deliver a finished product with a high degree of accuracy in the shortest time possible. Maple Leaf Construction is a leader in environmental recycling. We utilize concrete recycling and have developed an expertise in the use of recycled subbase, base and asphalt products.

Maple Leaf Construction takes pride in utilizing advanced, grade-controlled electronics in equipment such as asphalt pavers and concrete finishers. In addition, we employ GPS-guided technology for excavation and base preparation processes. These innovations enable our highly skilled team to complete construction projects with exceptional precision and efficiency, ensuring a high-quality finished product in the shortest time possible.

Furthermore, Maple Leaf Construction is recognized as a leader in sustainable construction practices, particularly in environmental recycling. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through the use of concrete recycling techniques. Over the years, we have developed extensive expertise in incorporating recycled materials—such as subbase, base, and asphalt—into our construction projects. This not only supports eco-friendly practices but also promotes cost-effective and resource-conscious solutions in the industry.
